Frequently Asked Questions

Is OfferLab a pyramid scheme or MLM?
No. OfferLab is a legitimate collaborative commerce platform. The 2% referral commission rewards you for introducing others to a valuable tool, but there's no requirement to recruit, no inventory to purchase, and no multi-level compensation structure.
Do I need technical skills to use OfferLab?
Basic technical literacy is helpful, but OfferLab is designed for marketers, not developers. The drag-and-drop interface and pre-built templates mean you can create functional funnels without coding knowledge.
What happens if OfferLab shuts down?
Because fulfillment happens on your existing platform (ClickFunnels, GHL, etc.), you won't lose customers or products. You'd simply lose the collaboration features and commission automation.
Can I promote OfferLab offers through any traffic source?
Generally yes, but review each offer's promotional guidelines. Most prohibit spam, fraudulent claims, and trademark bidding. Ethical promotion through content marketing, email lists, social media, and paid advertising is encouraged.
How quickly do I receive commissions?
OfferLab processes commissions on a rolling basis. Most users receive payments 30 days after transactions complete, allowing for refund periods. Payment thresholds and schedules depend on your account status.
Can I use OfferLab without my own product?
Absolutely. Many successful OfferLab users are pure affiliates who promote other creators' offers. You don't need your own product to build profitable funnels or earn referral commissions.
What's the difference between OfferLab and ClickFunnels?
ClickFunnels is a funnel builder where you create landing pages and sales processes. OfferLab is a collaboration platform that works with ClickFunnels and other builders. Think of ClickFunnels as the construction tool and OfferLab as the partnership management system.
